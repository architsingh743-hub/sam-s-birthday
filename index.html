🌙 Our Story So Far

Class 7 · The Merge

✨ The First "Hi"

Do you remember when our sections got merged in Class 7? New classmates, new faces, a whole room of people we didn't know yet. 😅

And out of all those unfamiliar faces, you were the one who actually said "Hi" first. 👋 Such a small thing, but somehow it stuck with me — I remember thinking, "okay, who is this girl with the short hair and the smile that stands out from the crowd?" ✂️✨

A Little Later

🧲 Opposites, Apparently

The more I got to know you, the more obvious it became — class topper 🏆, confident, always first to volunteer for school activities 🎤. And then there was me, doing the exact opposite. 😅

Turns out that's basically the plot of every good friendship — two completely different people who somehow just work. Unlike poles attract, remember? 🧲

A Quiet Moment

🌧️ The First Time I Saw You Cry

I remember the first time I saw you upset — you were missing an old friend. 💔 I didn't know you that well yet, but it still got to me a little. Funny how that works.

An Afternoon

🍦 The Orange Ice Cream

I remember sharing an orange ice cream with you and walking part of the way to your place. 🚶‍♀️🚶 Simple afternoon, nothing dramatic.

And then — for reasons that are still a mystery to me — the next day you said we wouldn't talk after school for a while. 🤷 No idea what happened there, honestly. Still funny to think about. 😅

First Chat

📱 "Hlwoo"

Then one random day, while I was just playing a game at home, a message popped up out of nowhere: "Hlwoo" 💬 I remember being genuinely surprised — first time anyone had messaged me like that.

You asked which show I'd recommended at school — School Friends 🎬 — and that turned into our first proper chat. And for some reason we kept it painfully formal and in English for almost a month. 😂

WhatsApp Era

📱 Status Updates & Group Chats

That's also when I started actually using WhatsApp statuses. 😅 We ended up in the same groups too — Matlabi Duniya and Who Cares — peak Class 7 energy. 👥😂

The Turning Point

🌧️ The Post

And then came the day I'd rather not relive in detail. I shared a post in Who Cares, something happened after that, and things changed pretty quickly. 💭 Honestly, still one memory I'd rather leave a little blurry.

Two Years

📵 The Long Quiet

After that, you didn't talk to me for almost two years. 📵 We barely spoke at school. I'd be lying if I said I didn't check our chat more times than I'd like to admit, just to see if anything had changed. 😅

Looking back, I'm genuinely sorry for whatever I did that started all that. 💜 Some memories teach you things the slow way — and that's how Class 7 wrapped up for us.

Meanwhile

🎤 The Annual Function

You did the anchoring at the Annual Function 🎤 — and absolutely nailed it, after what looked like a stressful month of prep. I was somewhere in the background doing something equally chaotic. 😂 You did so well that day. 👏

19 October 2025

💬 "Hlo bhai"

And then, out of nowhere — during the Exhibition prep, while I was on a call with Vipul planning our project — a message landed: "Hlo bhai" 💬

I genuinely froze for a second, then double-checked it was really you. 😳 Honestly one of the more memorable days of the year — and the start of us actually being friends again. 🎉

Since Then

🌸 Bit by Bit

Things have been getting better ever since — bit by bit. 💜 Sure, there've been a few misunderstandings and the occasional dramatic silent treatment 🌧️😅, but every one of those somehow made the friendship a little sturdier.
